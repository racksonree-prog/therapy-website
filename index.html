Meet Kasha

Compassionate counseling rooted in hope and understanding.

Welcome! I’m Kasha Luckner, a Licensed Professional Counselor who believes that no one should have to face life’s challenges alone. My goal is to provide a safe, supportive space where you can feel comfortable being yourself, sharing your story, and working toward the life you want.

As a cancer survivor myself, I understand firsthand how overwhelming a health journey can be — not just physically, but emotionally and spiritually as well. That experience has given me a deep sense of empathy and a genuine desire to help others find hope and healing through difficult seasons.

Approach

Counseling is a partnership.

Whether you’re feeling stuck, struggling with anxiety or depression, processing past experiences, or navigating a new chapter in life, I’m here to walk alongside you with compassion, encouragement, and practical support.

My Christian faith shapes the way I view hope, healing, and resilience, but I welcome people from all backgrounds and beliefs. I meet each client exactly where they are — with no judgment, no pressure, and no expectations beyond showing up as your authentic self.
